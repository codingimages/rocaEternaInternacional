Who we are?

Roca Eterna International Church was founded by pastors Jorge and Esperanza Campos on 2015 following God's call to serve the nations.

Pastors Jorge and Esperanza Campos love God and so their call to their life. They work hard every single day to for the lord.

Our primary focus is the gospel, to make disciples and get them ready to serve from our community to the nations.

We are located on1611 Briggs Chaney Rd, Silver Spring MD, 20905 and we hope to see you soon.


Our Vision


We are a visionary church. We are focused and ready to serve out community and the nations where the Lord want to bring us to help accoding to his will.


Our Mission


To reach the lives, make disciples and Jesus followers to send them to the nations and continue expanding the gospel.
